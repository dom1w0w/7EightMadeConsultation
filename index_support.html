FAQ

Common questions.

Do you replace our CRM?
No. We build assistants and automations that sit beside your stack. Keep your CRM—we help it work harder without touching your existing data architecture.
What about data privacy?
We use least-privilege access, your credential vault, optional self-hosting, and short-lived tokens. Your data stays where it belongs.
How fast is a pilot?
24–72 hours once access is granted. Smaller scope means faster ship. We scope carefully so we can move quickly.
Can you train our team?
Yes. A short training session plus a living SOP document is included in most builds. We want your team to be self-sufficient.
What if we only need one small thing?
The Starter package is built exactly for that—one focused use-case, scoped tightly, shipped fast. No overselling, no unnecessary scope.
Do you work with enterprise teams?
Yes. For larger or more complex needs, reach out directly at contact@7eightmade.com and we'll put together a custom scope and proposal.
